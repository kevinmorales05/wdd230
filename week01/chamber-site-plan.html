Our Purpose

The Quito Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to fostering a thriving business ecosystem in the city. Its purpose is to connect and empower local businesses, advocating for their interests, and providing essential resources. By facilitating collaboration and offering support services, the Chamber aims to enhance the competitiveness of its members. Through proactive engagement with government bodies and dynamic initiatives, it contributes to the economic development of Quito, creating a conducive environment for sustainable business growth.
